Vinu Philipose

An experienced HR professional from Palakkad, holding a Master's degree in Personnel Management and Industrial Relations from Bharathiar University, Coimbatore.

Over the years, Vinu has held key HR leadership positions in several esteemed organizations, most recently as Head of HR at United Breweries, Palakkad.

Beyond the boardroom, a lifelong passion for magic quietly transformed into a deep, disciplined obsession with the art of mentalism — performing at corporate events, private shows, and stages across Kerala.
